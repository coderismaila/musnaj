## Project Overview

The **300kVA, 33/0.400kV Distribution Substation Project** was executed for **ASAD CNG**, located at **Maraban Jos, Kaduna State**, to support critical gas processing and compression operations.

The project was designed to deliver a **dedicated, stable, and efficient power supply**, addressing voltage instability and frequent outages previously experienced due to shared network limitations.

MUSNAJ Electrical Concept Ltd executed the project on a **turnkey basis**, from engineering design through construction, installation, testing, and final commissioning.

---

## Scope of Work

The project encompassed all electrical and civil works required for a standard industrial distribution substation.

::tabs
  :::div{icon="i-lucide-list-checks" label="Project Components"}
  - Design and construction of a **300kVA, 33/0.400kV distribution substation**  
  - Installation of **300kVA power transformer**  
  - Construction of **reinforced concrete transformer plinth**  
  - Installation of **33kV incoming line structures and protection equipment**  
  - Installation of **LV panels, switchgear, and distribution boards**  
  - Installation of **earthing and lightning protection systems**  
  - Laying, termination, and jointing of **33kV and LV armored cables**  
  - **Testing, commissioning, and documentation**  
  - Final handover to **ASAD CNG operations team**
  :::

  :::div{icon="i-lucide-hard-hat" label="Safety and Compliance"}
  - Compliance with **NEMSA**, **COREN**, and distribution utility safety standards  
  - Deployment of **certified engineers and experienced field technicians**  
  - Strict enforcement of **PPE usage, grounding, and lockout–tagout procedures**  
  - Joint inspections and approvals with **client representatives**
  :::

  :::div{icon="i-lucide-clock" label="Timeline and Delivery"}
  - Project Duration: **8 Weeks**  
  - Start Date: **July 2024**  
  - Completion Date: **September 2024**  
  - Status: ✅ Completed and Energized  
  :::
::

## Project Impact

The commissioning of the substation significantly improved **operational reliability and energy efficiency** for ASAD CNG.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Stable and dedicated power supply for gas compression operations  
- Reduced voltage fluctuations and unplanned outages  
- Significant reduction in generator runtime and fuel consumption  
- Improved equipment performance and lifespan  
- Enhanced operational safety and reliability  

---

## Materials and Equipment Used

- 300kVA, 33/0.400kV power transformer  
- 33kV isolators, drop-out fuses, and lightning arrestors  
- LV switchgear panels and distribution boards  
- 33kV and LV armored power cables  
- Earthing rods, copper conductors, and earth enhancement compounds  
- Reinforced concrete transformer plinth  
- Electrical testing and commissioning equipment
