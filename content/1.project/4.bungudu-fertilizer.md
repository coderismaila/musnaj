## Project Overview

The **200kW/250kWh Solar Power System Project** was executed for **Bungudu Fertilizer Company**, Zamfara State, to support industrial operations with a **reliable, sustainable, and cost-efficient energy solution**.

The project was designed to significantly reduce dependence on diesel generators, lower operating costs, and improve power availability for critical production processes.

MUSNAJ Electrical Concept Ltd delivered the project on a **turnkey basis**, covering system design, procurement, installation, testing, and commissioning.

---

## Scope of Work

The project included the full deployment of a commercial-scale solar photovoltaic system with battery energy storage.

::tabs
  :::div{icon="i-lucide-list-checks" label="Project Components"}
  - Engineering design of a **200kW grid-tied solar PV system with battery backup**  
  - Supply and installation of **high-efficiency solar PV modules**  
  - Installation of **hybrid inverters and charge controllers**  
  - Installation of **250kWh lithium battery energy storage system**  
  - Construction of **solar mounting structures**  
  - Installation of **DC and AC cabling, protections, and isolators**  
  - Integration with existing **generator and utility power sources**  
  - **Testing, commissioning, and performance verification**  
  - System handover and **operator training**
  :::

  :::div{icon="i-lucide-hard-hat" label="Safety and Compliance"}
  - Compliance with **NEMSA**, **IEC**, and industry renewable energy standards  
  - Execution by **certified solar engineers and technicians**  
  - Strict enforcement of **PPE, electrical isolation, and grounding procedures**  
  - Final inspection and approval with **client representatives**
  :::

  :::div{icon="i-lucide-clock" label="Timeline and Delivery"}
  - Project Duration: **6 Weeks**  
  - Start Date: **September 2024**  
  - Completion Date: **November 2024**  
  - Status: ✅ Completed and Commissioned  
  :::
::

## Project Impact

The solar power system delivered measurable **operational, financial, and environmental benefits** to Bungudu Fertilizer Company.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Reliable renewable power supply for industrial operations  
- Over **40% reduction in diesel generator runtime**  
- Significant reduction in fuel and maintenance costs  
- Improved energy availability and production stability  
- Lower carbon emissions and enhanced sustainability performance  

---

## Materials and Equipment Used

- High-efficiency solar PV modules (200kW capacity)  
- Hybrid solar inverters and charge controllers  
- 250kWh lithium battery energy storage system  
- DC combiner boxes and AC distribution panels  
- Solar mounting structures and fasteners  
- DC and AC armored power cables  
- Earthing and lightning protection systems  
- Monitoring, testing, and commissioning equipment
