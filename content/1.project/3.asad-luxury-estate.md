## Project Overview

The **500kVA, 11/0.400kV Distribution Substation Project** was executed for **ASAD Luxury Estate**, located in **Unguwar Rimi, Kaduna**, to meet the power demands of a premium residential development.

The project was designed to provide **high-capacity, scalable, and reliable power distribution** to support both current and future estate load requirements.

---

## Scope of Work

::tabs
  :::div{icon="i-lucide-list-checks" label="Project Components"}
  - Construction of **500kVA distribution substation**  
  - Installation of **500kVA transformer**  
  - Installation of **11kV and LV switchgear**  
  - Cable laying, termination, and jointing  
  - Earthing and lightning protection  
  - Testing, commissioning, and energization
