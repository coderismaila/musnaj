## Project Overview

The **1000kVA, 33/0.400kV Distribution Substation & Control Room Project** was executed for **ASAD Lubricant Ltd**, a major industrial player located along Zaria Road, Kano.

The objective of the project was to provide a **dedicated, stable, and high-capacity power supply** to support continuous industrial production, eliminate voltage instability, and significantly reduce dependence on generators.

Musnaj Electrical Concept Ltd delivered the project on a **turnkey basis**, covering engineering design, civil works, electrical installations, testing, and commissioning in compliance with utility and regulatory standards.

---

## Scope of Work

The project covered the full development of a standard industrial distribution substation and control facility.

::tabs
  :::div{icon="i-lucide-list-checks" label="Project Components"}
  - Design and construction of a **1000kVA, 33/0.400kV distribution substation**  
  - Construction of a **reinforced concrete control room**  
  - Installation of **33kV incoming line structures and protection equipment**  
  - Installation of **1000kVA power transformer**  
  - Installation of **LV switchgear, panels, and distribution boards**  
  - Installation of **earthing and lightning protection systems**  
  - Laying, termination, and jointing of **33kV and LV cables**  
  - **Testing, commissioning, and documentation**  
  - Final handover to **ASAD Lubricant Ltd**
  :::

  :::div{icon="i-lucide-hard-hat" label="Safety and Compliance"}
  - Compliance with **NEMSA**, **COREN**, and utility safety requirements  
  - Execution by **certified engineers and experienced field technicians**  
  - Strict application of **PPE, lockout–tagout, and grounding procedures**  
  - Joint inspection and approval with **client representatives**
  :::

## Project Impact

The project significantly enhanced **power reliability and operational efficiency** for ASAD Lubricant Ltd by providing a dedicated high-capacity substation tailored to industrial load demand.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Stable and uninterrupted power supply for production processes  
- Improved voltage regulation and load handling  
- Significant reduction in generator usage and fuel costs  
- Enhanced safety and operational control through a dedicated control room  
- Improved equipment lifespan and reduced downtime  

---

## Materials and Equipment Used

- 1000kVA, 33/0.400kV power transformer  
- 33kV isolators, drop-out fuses, and lightning arrestors  
- LV switchgear panels and distribution boards  
- 33kV and LV armored power cables  
- Earthing rods, copper conductors, and earth enhancement compounds  
- Reinforced concrete foundations and control room structure  
- Electrical testing and commissioning equipment
