## Project Overview

The **300kVA, 11/0.400kV Distribution Substation Project** was executed for **ASAD Apartments**, located at **Kurmin Mashi, Kaduna State**, to provide a stable and dedicated electricity supply for a modern residential development.

The project addressed challenges related to voltage drops, load limitations, and dependence on shared distribution infrastructure by deploying a dedicated substation solution.

Musnaj Electrical Concept Ltd delivered the project on a **turnkey basis**, covering engineering design, construction, installation, testing, and commissioning.

---

::tabs
  :::div{icon="i-lucide-list-checks" label="Project Components"}

  - Construction of **transformer plinth and fencing**  
  - Installation of **11kV protection equipment**  
  - Installation of **LV panels and distribution boards**
