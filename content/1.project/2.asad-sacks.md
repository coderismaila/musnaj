## Project Overview

The **1000kVA, 33/0.400kV Distribution Substation & Control Room Project** was executed for **ASAD Sacks/CNG Ltd**, located along Zaria Road, Kano State, to support continuous industrial production and compressed natural gas (CNG) operations.

The project was designed to deliver a **dedicated, high-capacity, and reliable power supply**, addressing voltage instability, frequent outages, and heavy load demands associated with industrial manufacturing and gas processing.

MUSNAJ Electrical Concept Ltd delivered the project on a **turnkey basis**, covering engineering design, civil works, electrical installations, testing, and commissioning.

---

## Scope of Work

The project involved the complete development of a standard industrial distribution substation with an integrated control facility.

::tabs
  :::div{icon="i-lucide-list-checks" label="Project Components"}
  - Design and construction of a **1000kVA, 33/0.400kV distribution substation**  
  - Construction of a **reinforced concrete control room**  
  - Installation of **1000kVA power transformer**  
  - Installation of **33kV incoming line structures and protection equipment**  
  - Installation of **LV switchgear panels, MCCs, and distribution boards**  
  - Installation of **earthing and lightning protection systems**  
  - Laying, termination, and jointing of **33kV and LV armored cables**  
  - **Testing, commissioning, and documentation**  
  - Final handover to **ASAD Sacks/CNG Ltd operations team**
  :::

  :::div{icon="i-lucide-hard-hat" label="Safety and Compliance"}
  - Full compliance with **NEMSA**, **COREN**, and distribution utility standards  
  - Deployment of **certified engineers and experienced field technicians**  
  - Strict adherence to **PPE usage, grounding, and lockout–tagout procedures**  
  - Joint inspection and approval with **client and utility representatives**
  :::

  :::div{icon="i-lucide-clock" label="Timeline and Delivery"}
  - Project Duration: **10 Weeks**  
  - Start Date: **August 2024**  
  - Completion Date: **October 2024**  
  - Status: ✅ Completed and Energized  
  :::
::

## Project Impact

The commissioning of the substation significantly improved **power reliability, operational efficiency, and safety** for ASAD Sacks/CNG Ltd.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Stable and uninterrupted power supply for industrial and CNG operations  
- Improved voltage regulation and load handling capacity  
- Significant reduction in generator runtime and fuel costs  
- Enhanced safety and centralized system control via the control room  
- Improved operational efficiency and reduced downtime  

---

## Materials and Equipment Used

- 1000kVA, 33/0.400kV power transformer  
- 33kV isolators, drop-out fuses, and lightning arrestors  
- LV switchgear panels, MCCs, and distribution boards  
- 33kV and LV armored power cables  
- Earthing rods, copper conductors, and earth enhancement compounds  
- Reinforced concrete transformer foundation and control room structure  
- Electrical testing and commissioning equipment
