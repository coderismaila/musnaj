## Project Overview

Musnaj Electrical Concept Ltd executed comprehensive **electrical infrastructure works** for **ASAD Medics MC**, a healthcare facility in **Kaduna State**, aimed at ensuring reliable and safe power supply for medical operations.

Given the critical nature of healthcare services, the project emphasized **power reliability, safety, and compliance** with applicable electrical standards.

---

## Scope of Work

::tabs
  :::div{icon="i-lucide-list-checks" label="Project Components"}
  - Electrical wiring for clinical and support areas  
  - Installation of **distribution boards and switchgear**  
  - Power distribution for medical equipment  
  - Installation of **earthing and bonding systems**  
  - Functional testing and commissioning  
  :::

  :::div{icon="i-lucide-hard-hat" label="Safety and Compliance"}
  - Compliance with healthcare electrical safety requirements  
  - Proper segregation of critical and non-critical circuits  
  - Testing and verification before handover
