## Project Overview

Musnaj Electrical Concept Ltd was awarded an **Official Works Order** by **Kano Electricity Distribution Plc (KEDCO)** for the **procurement of distribution materials** required for the rehabilitation of selected **33kV and 11kV feeders** across Kano network segments.

This engagement demonstrates Musnaj’s capability to support **utility-scale distribution projects** in collaboration with a licensed DISCO.
